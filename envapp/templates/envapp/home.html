Welcome to the Procrastinators project on sustainability!

About us:

We are a small group of Exeter University students and we have come up with a way to not only encourage students to drink more water, but also to help reduce plastic waste.
Water is the most essential nutrient for life, but its importance is overlooked. It is an essential nutrient required for all body tissues and it is the largest single component of the body - [PMC PubMed Central - Click here for more information].
With Sip & Save, our goal is to educate students on the importance of staying hydrated, but mainly the importance of plastic waste and how with little effort we can all help!
